SAHARA

The Sahara Desert is the world's largest hot desert, spanning across northern Africa. It covers an area of approximately 9.2 million square kilometers.

WESTERN DESERT

The Western Desert is a large arid region in North Africa,
characterized by sand dunes and rocky terrain.

LIBYAN DESERT

The Libyan Desert, also known as the Western Desert,
is a vast desert region located in northern Africa.
